Available Scholarships

Merit-Based Scholarship

This scholarship is awarded to students who demonstrate exceptional academic performance.

  • Eligibility: Minimum GPA of 3.5
  • Coverage: Up to 50% of course fees
  • Deadline: December 31, 2023

Need-Based Scholarship

This scholarship is designed for students who require financial assistance to pursue their education.

  • Eligibility: Proof of financial need
  • Coverage: Up to 75% of course fees
  • Deadline: January 15, 2024

Women in Tech Scholarship

This scholarship encourages women to pursue careers in technology and STEM fields.

  • Eligibility: Female students enrolled in tech courses
  • Coverage: Up to 100% of course fees
  • Deadline: February 1, 2024

Diversity Scholarship

This scholarship promotes diversity and inclusion in the tech industry.

  • Eligibility: Underrepresented groups in tech
  • Coverage: Up to 50% of course fees
  • Deadline: March 1, 2024
