Admission Guidelines

Your step-by-step guide to applying for college admissions.

Admission Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • High school transcripts
  • Standardized test scores (SAT/ACT)
  • Letters of recommendation
  • Personal statement or essay
  • Application fee

Steps to Apply

  • Research colleges and programs
  • Prepare required documents
  • Submit your application online
  • Pay the application fee
  • Track your application status
  • Attend interviews (if required)

Important Deadlines

  • Early Decision: November 1
  • Regular Decision: January 15
  • Financial Aid: February 1
  • Scholarship Applications: March 1

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the application fee?

The application fee varies by college but typically ranges from $50 to $100.

Can I apply for financial aid?

Yes, most colleges offer financial aid. Submit the FAFSA or CSS Profile to apply.

What if I miss the deadline?

Late applications may be accepted on a case-by-case basis. Contact the admissions office for details.
