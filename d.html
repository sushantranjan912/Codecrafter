Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Code Crafter and its offerings.

FAQs

What is Code Crafter?

Code Crafter is an online learning platform offering 20+ in-demand courses in Web Development, Data Science, AI, Cybersecurity, and more.

How do I sign up for a course?

You can sign up by creating an account on our platform, browsing the courses, and enrolling in the one that suits your needs.

Are there any prerequisites for the courses?

Most courses are beginner-friendly, but some advanced courses may require prior knowledge. Check the course description for details.

Do you offer scholarships?

Yes, we offer merit-based, need-based, and diversity scholarships. Visit the Scholarship Information page for details.

Can I access course materials after completion?

Yes, you get lifetime access to course materials, including videos, projects, and resources.

Are the courses self-paced?

Yes, all courses are self-paced, allowing you to learn at your own convenience.

Do you provide certificates?

Yes, you will receive a certificate of completion for each course you finish.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, and other popular payment methods.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all courses.

Do you offer career support?

Yes, we provide career guidance, resume reviews, and interview preparation to help you land your dream job.

Are there any free courses available?

Yes, we offer free introductory courses for beginners. Check the course catalog for details.

How do I contact support?

You can reach out to our support team via email at support@codecrafter.com or through the Contact Us page.

Can I switch courses after enrolling?

Yes, you can switch courses within 7 days of enrollment. Contact support for assistance.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes, we offer discounts for group enrollments. Contact us for more information.

What is the duration of each course?

Course durations vary, but most courses can be completed in 4-12 weeks, depending on your pace.
